About Us

Coaching small groups of players to strategize, encourage critical thinking, problem-solving and promote an inclusive cross-cultural environment.

We are a group of students and teachers who enjoy playing chess. We are always looking for new members to join our club. We get together every week to play chess and learn new moves. In the future, we will be holding virtual or one-on-one chess tournaments.
